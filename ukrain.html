Ukraine Crisis Relief Fund

Helping the people of Ukraine through their time of crisis.

Summary

The Ukraine Crisis Relief Fund is dedicated to providing emergency relief to those affected by the ongoing conflict in Ukraine. Funds will go toward food, shelter, medical care, and more.

Challenge & Solution

The ongoing conflict in Ukraine has caused widespread devastation, leaving many without basic necessities. By mobilizing funds, we aim to provide immediate relief to those in need through food packages, medical supplies, and emergency shelter.

Long-term Impact

The long-term goal is to help displaced families rebuild their lives, access education, and achieve self-sufficiency once the conflict subsides. This initiative will also create sustainable support for Ukraine's recovery.

Resources

All resources raised will be distributed to local NGOs operating in Ukraine, with a focus on healthcare, food security, shelter, and education for displaced families.

Goals

  • Provide immediate food and medical aid to 10,000 displaced individuals.
  • Set up 5 emergency shelters for families in the most affected areas.
  • Partner with local NGOs to deliver long-term support for education and healthcare.

Timeline

  • Launch fundraiser: February 2025
  • Start food and shelter distribution: March 2025
  • Ongoing support: Continuous throughout the crisis
