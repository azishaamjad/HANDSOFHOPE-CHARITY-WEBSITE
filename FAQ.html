Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about how Hands of Hope supports those in need.

How does Hands of Hope help the homeless?

We provide essential supplies, shelter, and medical care to those experiencing homelessness. Our outreach programs work to assist them in finding stable housing and employment.

How do you help feed the hungry?

Through our food distribution programs, we provide nutritious meals to individuals and families who are struggling with hunger, especially in underprivileged areas.

What is your support for street vendors?

We financially assist vendors and individuals selling items on the streets, helping them maintain their livelihoods and support their families. Our goal is to help them sustain a stable income.

How do you identify those in extreme poverty?

We visit underdeveloped areas and communities to identify those living in extreme poverty. Our team conducts outreach programs to assess their needs and provide necessary resources.

How do you fundraise for medical treatments?

We organize events and campaigns to raise funds for medical treatments, focusing on those who cannot afford necessary healthcare. These funds go directly to those in need of urgent medical assistance.

How does Hands of Hope address global issues?

We organize global events aimed at raising awareness and funds for issues such as climate change, poverty alleviation, and healthcare access. Our goal is to create a positive impact on a global scale.
