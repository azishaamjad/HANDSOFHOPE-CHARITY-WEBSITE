Summary

This initiative aims to provide foster care, education, and a safe home for children who have been abandoned or are living in poor conditions in Thailand.

Challenge & Solution

The challenge is the increasing number of children in need of a safe home and education. By partnering with local foster care centers, we aim to provide a better future through foster care programs, education, and emotional support.

Long-term Impact

This initiative will provide children with opportunities for better education, stable living conditions, and emotional well-being, helping them grow into successful and productive individuals.

Resources

We rely on donations, volunteers, and collaboration with local foster care facilities. All resources will go toward providing food, shelter, education, and emotional support for these children.

Goals

  • Provide shelter and care for 100 children in the first year.
  • Raise funds for educational supplies and school fees.
  • Establish partnerships with local schools to integrate children into mainstream education.

Timeline

  • Launch fundraiser: January 2025
  • Start foster care program: March 2025
  • First graduation of children in the program: December 2026
